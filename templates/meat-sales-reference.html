Current Offer

Use this as the internal truth while reviewing Beacon posts and SAM leads.

Pilot product Half carcass with Set A / Family Freezer Pack as the public default.
Manual override Full carcass can be offered to known/trusted customers when stock and fulfilment allow it.
Later products Custom cuts and assisted slaughter stay later add-ons until the first standard meat cycles are stable.
Launch area Zone 1: Riversdale, Albertinia, and Still Bay unless owner approves otherwise.

Cut Sets

These are product descriptions, not a promise of exact final weights or final availability.

Set A: Family Freezer Pack Pork chops; leg portions or roasts; shoulder roasts; belly strips; ribs; mince or stew meat; bones for soup or stock.
Set B: Braai Pack Chops; rashers or belly strips; ribs; shoulder steaks; sosatie or stew cubes; mince or sausage meat option.
Set C: Lean Pack Lean chops; leg steaks; lean shoulder cuts; mince; stew cubes; fewer fatty belly cuts.
Set D: Budget Bulk Pack Larger roasting cuts; mince; stew meat; soup bones; shoulder; mixed chops; less detailed trimming.

Pricing And Payment

Planning figures for owner review. Final quotes still depend on backend gates and owner approval.

Standard half/full carcass Planning price around R130/kg VAT-inclusive at launch.
Custom cut lane Later target around R145-R150/kg with higher admin and deposit requirements.
Deposit Standard carcass orders use 50% deposit of the estimated VAT-inclusive total.
Final invoice Final balance uses actual packed weight. Balance must clear before delivery or collection.

Non-Negotiable Gates

These rules protect the farm from overpromising and cash-flow mistakes.

No slaughter without money No pig is slaughtered for meat sales unless it is pre-sold and the required deposit is bank-confirmed.
POP is not payment Proof of payment is evidence only. Bank-confirmed money unlocks the next gate.
No invented promises SAM, Beacon, Butcher, and Ledger must not invent price, availability, delivery, booking, or payment confirmation.
Demand cap Sell only what the farm can safely fulfil from pigs over 60kg and approved operational capacity.
