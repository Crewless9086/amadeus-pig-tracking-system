# Owner Charl

Charl is the owner and final decision authority.

The system exists to help Charl see the truth, decide faster, and build income-generating operations without losing control.

Charl approves:

- mission authority levels;
- final release;
- business rules;
- customer-send authority;
- public-post authority;
- payment/deposit/reservation actions;
- farm lifecycle changes;
- legal/privacy policy;
- destructive or production data-changing actions.
