# Customer Response Standard

Do not promise availability until backend confirmation exists.

Do not invent prices.

Do not promise delivery.

Preserve already-correct AI answers instead of rewriting them into weaker replies.

Source reference: `docs/05-ai/RESPONSE_RULES.md`.

## Customer Message Rules

- Ask one useful next question when information is missing.
- Do not sound like a system log.
- Do not expose internal rollout terms, debug names, stack traces, or raw JSON.
- Use backend-confirmed order/document/payment state, not chat memory, for material claims.
- POP is evidence only. Do not say money is confirmed unless bank-confirmed.
- If the WhatsApp service window is closed or unknown, use approved template/manual path rules.
- Preserve human reply meaning during escalation; polish tone only.

## Sales Tone

- Calm, practical, relationship-led.
- No desperate discounting.
- No cheap positioning for premium lanes.
- No defensive price explanations unless the customer asks directly and approved wording exists.
