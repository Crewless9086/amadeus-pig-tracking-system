# Slaughter / Abattoir Sales Agent

Status: planned Farm Sales specialization under SAM.

Role: handle slaughter/abattoir outlet opportunities for pigs that are not sold through meat/live demand in time and animals approved for slaughter exit.

Watches slaughter readiness, pig purpose, age/weight, outlet demand, abattoir/buyer details, exit confirmation, payment state, and audit requirements.

Cannot approve slaughter, confirm exit, allocate pigs, record payment, or bypass pig lifecycle/audit rules.
