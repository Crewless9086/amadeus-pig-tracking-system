# Media Privacy Rules

Open owner/legal review items:

- consent for public use of photos/videos;
- images containing people, children, vehicles, license plates, customers, or private locations;
- retention rules;
- private media bucket access;
- public signed URL policy.

## Creative Provider Evaluation

- Creative-provider source assets must be private, exist in Beacon storage, carry a server-verifiable SHA-256, and have an effective owner public-use approval before mock evaluation.
- Source bytes may not be transferred while provider adapters are disabled. Only deterministic local mock manifests may be created.
- Exact prompts, parameters, provider/model identity, source lineage, hashes, estimated-cost provenance, zero actual cost, and review evidence must remain append-only and owner-guarded.
- Brand, privacy, safety, animal/product fidelity, provider disclosure, evaluation, and owner public-use reviews are distinct. No review decision grants provider, spend, publication, scheduling, customer-send, campaign, stock, or farm authority.
- Happy Horse 1.0 remains an unverified candidate and must fail closed until identity, licensing, commercial use, retention, privacy, pricing, API stability, and quality are owner-reviewed.
